Tools of Architecture

Architects use a variety of tools to design and build buildings. Some of the most common tools used in architecture include:

  • Drawing tools, such as pencils, pens, and markers
  • Rulers and measuring tapes
  • Levels and plumb bobs
  • T-squares and triangles
  • Protractors and compasses
  • Computers and software

These tools help architects to create accurate drawings and plans, and to ensure that buildings are built to the correct specifications.
